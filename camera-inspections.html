Answers You Can See

WHAT A CAMERA REVEALS

A drain that’s running today doesn’t mean your pipe is healthy. Here’s what we look for.

Cracks & Breaks

Even small cracks let water and roots in. We find them before they become collapses.

Tree Root Intrusion

Roots seek water and slowly destroy pipes. We spot the intrusion points exactly.

Scale & Corrosion

Mineral buildup narrows your pipes over time. We measure how much you’ve lost.

Offset Joints

Shifting ground moves pipe sections apart, creating leaks and catching debris.

Bellies & Sags

Low spots in your pipe trap waste and cause recurring clogs that snaking can’t fix.

Collapsed Pipe

A fully failed section — rare, but critical to find before it causes major backups.
