Who We Are

WHAT WE’RE BUILT ON

FAMILY-OWNED. Rooter Champion is a family-owned plumbing and drain company based in LA & Orange County, California. We’re not a franchise and we’re not a call center — every job is run by the same family that built this company from the ground up, and that shows in how we treat your home.

LOCALLY ROOTED. We live where we work. Our technicians know every LA & OC neighborhood, every city’s permit office, and every quirk of the homes we service — from La Habra to San Clemente. When you call, you reach someone who’s worked under the same kind of home you’re standing in.

CERTIFIED & EQUIPPED. We invest in the tools most rooter companies skip: high-definition sewer cameras, 4,000 PSI hydro jetters, trenchless lining rigs, and sewer-robotics cutters. Every tech on our crew is certified to operate what they run — no apprentices figuring it out on your job.

That’s what being a Rooter Champion means — doing the harder job, the smarter way, for the people who live next door.
