When Robotics Are Needed

3 KEY APPLICATIONS

Robotic cutting solves problems that snaking, jetting, and excavation can’t — with surgical precision.

Use 01 · Post-Lining

REOPEN LATERALS

After a CIPP liner cures, branch lines (laterals) from sinks, showers, and toilets are sealed off. Our robotic cutter carefully reopens each connection — precise and liner-safe.

Why it matters:
  • Restore all branch connections
  • Zero damage to new liner
  • Precision opening to exact size
  • Full flow restored to every fixture
Use 02 · Heavy Obstructions

TOUGH REMOVALS

Hardened scale, calcium, concrete, grout, protruding tap connections — obstructions that snaking and jetting can’t touch. The robot cuts through with controlled force.

What it handles:
  • Calcified mineral buildup
  • Hardened tree roots
  • Grout & concrete intrusions
  • Protruding tap connections
Use 03 · Precision Work

SURGICAL CLEANUP

When you need precision — removing a specific obstacle without damaging the pipe, clearing a tight spot, or reaching where other tools can’t. Real-time camera control.

The advantage:
  • Real-time camera guidance
  • Surgical-level precision
  • Protects surrounding pipe
  • Avoids full excavation

Why Precision Matters

SEWER ROBOTICS IS AN ART.

A robotic cutting head operates inside a live sewer line — millimeters from the CIPP liner that’s protecting your pipes. One wrong move damages the liner and creates a bigger problem than you started with.

That’s why this work needs a specialist. Our certified technicians control the cutter under live camera, positioning it precisely at the lateral tie-in to reopen the connection without touching the surrounding liner.

It’s not a job you hand to the new guy — it takes years of experience, steady hands, and professional equipment to get it right every time.
