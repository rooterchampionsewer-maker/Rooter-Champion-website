Choose The Right Service

3 LEVELS OF CARE

Different blockages require different tools. Here’s how we match the right service to your pipe’s condition.

Level 01 · Light Buildup

DRAIN CLEANING

For everyday clogs and lighter buildups. Uses mechanical snaking or augering to clear hair, grease, food debris, and soft blockages from kitchen, bathroom, and floor drains.

Best for:
  • Slow kitchen or bathroom drains
  • Minor to moderate clogs
  • Hair, grease & food buildup
  • First-time drain issues
Level 02 · Tough Buildup

HYDRO JETTING

Industrial-grade water pressure up to 4,000 PSI blasts away stubborn, deep buildup. Clears what snaking can’t — including grease layers, years of scale, and tree root intrusion.

Best for:
  • Heavy grease & scale
  • Root intrusion
  • Recurring main line clogs
  • Commercial & restaurant drains
Level 03 · Severe Buildup

DESCALING

Mechanical descaling removes years of rust, calcium, and mineral buildup that have narrowed your pipe’s diameter. Restores pipes to full capacity without replacement.

Best for:
  • Rust & mineral deposits
  • Cast iron & older pipes
  • Reduced pipe diameter
  • Prep before pipe lining

Not sure which one you need? Call 714-305-9263 — we’ll run a camera inspection first to diagnose the right solution.

Is This Right For You?

SIGNS YOU NEED DRAIN CLEANING

If you’re experiencing any of these issues, drain cleaning is likely what you need.

Slow-Draining Sinks

Water takes longer than usual to drain in bathroom or kitchen sinks.

Gurgling Sounds

Strange noises from drains indicate partial blockages trapping air.

Multiple Slow Drains

When more than one drain is slow, the main sewer line is likely clogged.

Foul Odors

Bad smells from drains mean buildup or sewer gas escaping.

Frequent Clogs

Recurring clogs often signal deeper buildup that needs professional attention.

Water Backup

Water backing up into tubs or floor drains is a sign of a major blockage.
